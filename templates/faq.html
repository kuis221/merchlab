FAQ page - FAQ & Support for AccelerList

General questions

All general questions about AccelerList.
What re-pricing program do you recommend?
Repriceit.com is a really great program. It is what we use on our personal accounts. It starts at $9.99/month. You can also leverage the “Match Low Price” feature directly on your Amazon Seller Central account, however that is usually matching against MF sellers so it’s best to use a re-pricing software. You can select up to 200 items at a time and automatically match the lowest priced seller. You can match the lowest FBA or Merchant Fulfilled seller.
Can I label the items myself?
Sure thing. You can print the labels from your Amazon Seller Central account with the Avery 5160 labels, 30 at a time. They will print out in the same order that you listed the books. We recommend using Amazon to label your books or any product you’re listing with AccelerList as it is another way to improve your work flows.
What are the requirements/costs of the Amazon Label Service?
All items or products must have a barcode, and it costs 20 cents each for Amazon to label them.
Can I use AccelerList on more than one computer or any browser?
Yes and no. We will be supporting “employee accounts” very soon. However, you can share your login information with other owners of your business and AccelerList will work in ANY browser that’s connected to the internet.
Can I change the information after it is scanned into AccelerList?
Yes, you can edit the individual comments, price, SKU number, and condition.
I am a Merchant-Fulfilled seller. Can I still use AccelerList?
You betcha! As a matter of fact, AccelerList works very well for listing merchant-fulfilled items.
